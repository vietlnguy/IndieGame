Player (swordsman/melee):
- Slash
- Charge Strike - Big damage, but always attacks second.
- Dazing Blow - 50% to daze for 2 turns
- Final Gambit - Huge damage but cannot act next turn.

Elani (dancer rogue/melee):
- Backstab
- Punishment - Bonus damage if enemy is buffed.
- Babydoll Eyes - Set Vulnerable (reduces DEF and RES)
- Climax - (Instantly kills if brought below 20% health)

Astrid (ranger/ranged):
- Bow Shot
- Power Draw - Bonus damage
- Cripple - 100% chance to cripple for one turn
- Headshot - Guaranteed to critically strike

Celeste (priestess/melee):
- Heal
- Mana Restore - Restore target mana
- Cure - Restores large hp and cures debuffs  
- Ilvera's Protection - Completely heal/mana and set Invulnerable for 1 turn

Gerard (general/melee):
- Thrust
- Triumphant Shout - 75% to taunt enemy.
- Shield Bash - Extra damage per DEF
- Last Stand - Set Undying user cannot drop below 1 hp this turn.

Ivy (nature mage/ranged):
- Vine Lash
- Noxious Fumes - 75 percent chance to poison
- Essence Drain - Restore mana equal to half damage
- Spore Burst - Big damage if target is poisoned, removes poison.

Katherine (cavalier/melee):
- Trample
- Flank Attack - Bonus damage if enemy cannot attack back.
- Flame Charge - Resisted by RES rather than DEF.
- Stampede - Does not end turn.

Lucas (pugilist/melee):
- Double punch
- Blurry Fist - 50% chance to confuse for 2 turns
- Hero Spirit - Power is doubled when user is below 50%
- Intinct -  Enter flow state. Speed doubled.

Penelope (instrument/ranged) :
- Split Chord - Restore hp and mana
- Power Chord - Set Charged (Next attack does double damage.)
- Rejuvenate - Allow ally to take another turn.
- Crecendo - Set boosted (All primary stats boosted)

Maeve (Hemomancer/ranged) :
- Blood Magik
- Life Drain - Heal for the same amount
- Sacrifice - Transfer 25% current health to an ally.
