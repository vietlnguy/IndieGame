Chapter 3 - Lord Beesly, the Kind?:
- Intro: Mysterious figure is talking to Lord Beesly, demands submission and loyalty, otherwise there will be consequences. Enter Penelope, Gerard, and Katherine. Penelope demands
    demands to know from her father why he is terrorizing the people. Beesly tries to explain it is for her own good. Soldier enters saying party is there to speak with him. They say they
    know about the artifacts. Party arrives, asks to know what is going on. Beesly explains that the relics possess tremendous power and that the kingdom cannot risk the artifacts falling into
    the wrong hands. Beesly tries to recruit party to help, but party refuses. Beesly says he cannot allow them to leave. Party tries to escape.
- Outro: Party says they need to flee across the border into Seledo. Beesly talking to himself wishing them to be safe. 
- Post: With nowhere left to run, the party flees across the river border and through the mountains into Seledo.

Chapter 4 - The Oasis

- Outro: Elani thanks party for the help and asks to stay with them. Says she overheard two imperial guards talking about some ancient ruins they were going to search. Says its will be a big haul
    and they might find more artifacts.
- Post: Party heads toward ruins
