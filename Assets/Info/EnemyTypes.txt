Enemy Types and AI control:

- ranged
- melee
- roaming
- support
- hybrid

When enemy is not in in range to attack anyone:
- Should path to the closest player character


When enemy is in range to attack (in order of precedence):
- Should prioritize targeting players they can kill
- Should prioritize attacking melee characters if they themselves are ranged
- Should prioritize attacking ranged characters, if they themselves are melee.
- Should prioritize attacking the closest enemy
