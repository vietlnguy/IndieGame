Negative statuses:

Poisoned - Damage per turn
Dazed - Lowers ATK and INT
DONE - Crippled - Cannot move
Vulnerable - Lowers defense and resistance
Confused - Lowers skl
Taunted - Attack closest target


Positive statuses:

Charged - Next attack does 1.5x damage
Flowing - Boosted speed and skl.
Invigorated - Boosts all stats
Invincible - Negate all damage
Undying - Cannot be reduced below 1 health

Logic:

If an character is already buffed/debuffed: Applying it again only adds more turns. It does not stack
